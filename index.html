Bhai, chalein Coorg? ⛰️

🗺️ Itinerary — "Plan toh hai, follow nahi karenge"

Tap kisi bhi din pe — video chalega 🎬 (IDs config in script)

Day 1 · Arrive 🛬
▶

Madikeri pohonche, settle ho

Check-in @ estate homestay, Raja's Seat susset 🌄, Omkareshwara temple, dinner = pandi curry + akki roti. First photo ritual mandatory 📸

Day 2 · Falls + Elephants 💦
▶

Abbey Falls + Dubare camp

Abbey Falls (slippery hai, sambhal ke), Madikeri Fort, then Dubare elephant camp — haathi nahla, river rafting if guts hai. Evening: estate bonfire 🔥

Day 3 · Sunrise jeep 🌅
▶

Mandalpatti + coffee tour

4 AM uthna 😴 — Mandalpatti jeep ride, cloud-sea view. Wapis aake plantation walk, filter coffee tasting, lazy lunch.

Day 4 · Talakaveri 🛕
▶

Talakaveri + Nisargadhama

Kaveri origin pe darshan + Bhagamandala temple. Wapis aake Nisargadhama bamboo grove, hanging bridge, deer park. Shaam ko cafe-hop in Madikeri.

Day 5 · Bye 🥹
▶

"Ek aur din ruk jaate"

Late brunch, coffee + chocolate + honey shopping (sab ke liye), wapsi drive. Group photo with sad faces. Promise to plan next trip within 2 weeks (won't happen).
